Who We Are

An AI-First Company,
Built Over 16 Years

Infinity Global Solutions (IGS) is an AI and technology solutions company founded in 2009 at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Over 16 years, we have evolved from an embedded systems startup into a full-spectrum AI solutions provider operating across Singapore, Myanmar, and China.

Our core strength is the intersection of human expertise and machine intelligence. Through our Myanmar operations, we run one of Southeast Asia's largest AI data annotation and BPO centres — with a team of 2,000+ quality-trained specialists delivering high-accuracy training data for machine learning and computer vision models worldwide.

We are a recognised Alibaba.com Global Service Partner for Singapore and Myanmar, and an AliCloud Products & Services provider — connecting regional businesses to global cloud and AI infrastructure.
